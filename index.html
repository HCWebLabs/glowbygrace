My Story

Hi, I'm Grace

Beauty has always been my passion. What started as doing my friends' eyebrows in college turned into a calling I couldn't ignore. After years of training and perfecting my craft, I opened Glow by Grace to share that passion with you.

I believe beauty should feel effortless — enhancing what you already have, never covering it up. Every face tells a story. I'm here to help yours shine.

— Grace Monroe
