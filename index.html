My Story

Hi, I'm Grace

Beauty has always been my passion. What started as doing my friends' eyebrows in college turned into a calling I couldn't ignore. After years of training and perfecting my craft, I opened Glow by Grace to share that passion with you.

I believe that beauty should feel effortless. My approach is all about enhancing what you already have — not covering it up. Whether you're here for a subtle brow tint or a full lash transformation, you'll leave feeling like the best version of yourself.

Every face tells a story, and I'm here to help yours shine.

— Grace Monroe
